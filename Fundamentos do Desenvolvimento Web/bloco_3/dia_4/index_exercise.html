Fatos sobre o Stomatopoda

Stomatopoda ou Odontodactylus scyllarus

Odontodactylus scyllarus is the scientific name of Stomatopoda. While humans have three types of light-detecting cells, or photoreceptors, which are sensitive to red, green and blue light. This little shrimp has has anywhere from 12 to 16 different photoreceptors.
